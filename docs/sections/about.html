About

Built from nothing. Built to last.

Kleos began with zero skills and a single brand name — a word meaning "glory that outlives you" in both Greek and Sanskrit.

It is a dark, cinematic creative studio: design, video editing, and motion graphics. Restraint over noise. Atmosphere over flash.

The numbers came fast. The work is still being built — every single day.
