KLEOS — How to add your hero background videos (TWO videos, crossfading)
========================================================================
The hero now slowly crossfades between TWO looping videos.

1. Get TWO of your reels as .mp4 files (original exports are best).
   - Video 1 (e.g. BANKAI)
   - Video 2 (the Toji edit)
2. Keep each short (5-15 sec — they loop) and compress each UNDER 5 MB:
     https://www.freeconvert.com/video-compressor   (or HandBrake)
3. Name them EXACTLY:
     hero.mp4    <- shows first
     hero2.mp4   <- crossfades in (the Toji one)
4. Put BOTH in this folder (frontend/assets/).
5. (Optional) Add a still frame named  hero-poster.jpg  — shown before
   the videos load. A screenshot of either reel works.
6. Save, refresh the browser (Ctrl+Shift+R). Done.

TUNING
- Want each clip to stay longer/shorter before fading? Open animations.js,
  find setupHeroVideos(), change the 6000 (milliseconds = 6 sec).
- Want a slower/faster fade? Open style.css, find .hero-vid, change the
  "transition: opacity 1.5s" value.
- Video too bright / text hard to read? Open style.css, find #hero-overlay,
  raise the 0.6 / 0.75 / 0.92 numbers closer to 1 (darker).

NOTES
- Only ONE video added? It just shows that one (no crossfade) — still fine.
- NO videos added? The text hero shows on plain black — still works.
- Videos are muted and loop automatically (browsers require muted to autoplay).
