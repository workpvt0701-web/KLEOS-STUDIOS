The Brief

A cinematic character poster for Sōsuke Aizen — the strategist who planned every move. The design had to feel composed, cold, and inevitable.

The Process

Cormorant Garamond for the name, a desaturated palette with violet undertones, and the tagline 'Every move you made was a part of his plan' set small and quiet. Negative space does the heavy lifting.
